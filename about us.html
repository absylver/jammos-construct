Who We Are

Built for Reliable Project Delivery

Jammos Construction Nigeria Limited is a Nigerian engineering and construction company incorporated in Nigeria and registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) under RC 1043373.

Established in 2006, the company provides integrated engineering, procurement, and construction services across civil infrastructure, building construction, industrial installations, and telecommunications infrastructure projects.

Our multidisciplinary team combines engineering expertise, project management capability, and modern construction techniques to deliver projects safely, efficiently, and to the highest quality standards.

Jammos Construction Nigeria Limited

Jammos Construction Nigeria Limited is an indigenous construction, engineering, and project management company delivering infrastructure solutions that support industrial and economic development.

Since its establishment in 2006, the company has developed expertise across multiple engineering disciplines including civil engineering works, structural construction, industrial facilities, and telecommunications infrastructure deployment.

Through a commitment to engineering excellence, professional integrity, and innovation, Jammos Construction continues to deliver projects that meet client expectations while supporting sustainable development.
